## 🔄 Replacing Images

To use your own images:

1. Replace the Unsplash URLs with your image paths
2. Keep the same image dimensions for best results
3. Maintain the `loading="lazy"` attribute
4. Update alt text to match your images

Example:
```html
<!-- Before -->
<img src="https://images.unsplash.com/photo-xxx" alt="Description">

<!-- After -->
<img src="images/your-photo.jpg" alt="Your Description" loading="lazy">
```
